At Stone Town, the capital city of Zanzibar, one can find several historical structures dating back hundreds of years, including the House of Wonders, which was built during the Sultanate (Omani empire). You can go inside the building, hang out in the neighborhood, chat with locals, and buy some amazing African goods.
One of the world's most magnificent islands is Prison Island. This lovely location was intended to house infected people as captives, but it was never able to do so. It was then transformed into a tourist attraction. Renting a boat allows you to travel to the island, where you may swim at a gorgeous beach, snap pictures with turtles, and spend the night.
