Magnificent ancient architecture surround Madrid, a wonderful Spanish city to visit. You can look around the town, discover its history, and get to know hip Spanish people. Soccer enthusiasts should visit the legendary Real Madrid stadium.
One of the most important museums in all of Spain, Prado Museum Madrid (Museo Nacional del Prado) is known to have the most comprehensive collection of Spanish art and one of the most outstanding collections of European Art between the 12th century to the early 20th century. You can look at all the beautiful artwork and discover some historical events about Christianity, prehistoric Spain, and more.
Paella is unquestionably one of the most popular foods in Spain. It is made with soft rice, olives, lemon, and either chicken or seafood. But you should be aware that it's a bit salty. The free appetizers that come with bread, Spanish cheese, and the best olives in the world are what make this more intriguing.
